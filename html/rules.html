Rules

This game will test your habilities to recognize world-wide famous couples!


You should, based on the tips given in texts and images, reveal the names of the couples' members at each level (it is worth to mention it could be about a real or a fictional pair, including the ones in cartoons). After finding out the answer, insert their names in their respective boxes: if correct, you will advance to the next stage, totalizing 8 levels.

Note: insert their first names only.

Prove your romance!
